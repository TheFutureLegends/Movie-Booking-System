About Us

Our project is about an Online Booking website offers a quick and easy search for any kind of movies. The site will hold a wide array of movies for users to book and watch with ease. Users would either go to the Movies tab then choose one of the movies suggestion for quick booking. Each movie will have Seats information, Times, Theater location, Users can add/remove any booked events into their customizable Movie lists in their accounts.
We chose to make this system because it was the only suggestion we all agree on due to our past experience in Web Programming. We aim to make this system an user-friendly and interactive website that provides detailed information for all users.
